About Livity Wires

Securing your space. Defining your boundary.

Kenya's Trusted Wire Fencing Manufacturer

Livity Wires is a leading Kenyan manufacturer of high-quality wired fencing solutions. Based in Mombasa Road, Mlolongo, we specialize in producing durable chain link fences, galvanized barbed wire, and high-security razor wire that protect properties across East Africa.

With years of experience serving farmers, homeowners, schools, industrial facilities, and government institutions, we understand the unique security challenges faced by Kenyan property owners. Our products are designed to withstand the local climate while providing maximum protection.

From small residential plots to large commercial installations, Livity Wires delivers fencing solutions that combine strength, durability, and affordability.
